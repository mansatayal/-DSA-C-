Big O Notation :
- expresses the upper bound of an algorithm's time or space complexity 
- we mainly consider the worst case scenario of the algorithm to find it's complexity in terms of big o

TYPES:
- constant time complexity: O(1)
- linear time complexity: O(n)
- quadratic time complexity: O(n^2)
- polynomial time complexity: O(n ^ k)
- logarithmic time complexity: O(log n)
- logarithmic time complexity: O(n log n)
- exponential time complexity: O(2 ^ n)
- square root time complexity: O(root n)
- factorial time complexity: O(n!)     ->includes => permutations => travelling salesman problem
